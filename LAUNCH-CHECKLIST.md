# SUNBOUND BOHEME Launch Checklist

## Store Content

- Replace any remaining sample copy, example contact details, and placeholder social handles.
- Confirm every live product has final pricing, quantity, category, slug, and shipping settings.
- Check each product page for clear descriptions, condition notes, and accurate photos.
- Make sure there are no broken images in the shop or product pages.

## Orders & Fulfillment

- Confirm the admin route loads at `/alliesthrone` and still requires the family password.
- Verify orders show customer email, shipping address, notes, tracking, and status controls.
- Test moving an order from `New` to `Fulfilled` to `Shipped`.
- Decide on a returns workflow and who will monitor the support inbox daily.

## Email

- Confirm `support@sunboundboheme.com` sends and receives correctly in Zoho.
- Place one fresh test order and verify:
  - customer confirmation email arrives
  - internal order notification arrives
- Check spam/junk once before launch so you know how the messages land.

## Payments & Taxes

- Confirm Stripe is in the correct mode before launch.
- Re-enable Stripe automatic tax only after the business profile is fully configured.
- Review shipping preset rates in admin and adjust them against actual package costs.
- Run one final purchase test after any Stripe tax or shipping change.

## Customer Experience

- Review the home page, shop, product page, cart, policies, and about/contact pages on desktop.
- Review the same pages on mobile.
- Test search, category filters, add-to-cart, cart quantity changes, checkout, and cancel flow.
- Make sure sold-out items behave the way you want.

## Final Go-Live

- Remove any leftover non-public admin bookmarks or shared test links.
- Back up the current database before launch day.
- Double-check domain email delivery and access for everyone who needs it.
- Do one final end-to-end order from product page to inbox to admin order management.
